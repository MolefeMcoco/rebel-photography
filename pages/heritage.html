HEADWRAP SERIES

"The African headwrap is like a regal coronet, drawing the onlookers gaze up rather then down. African women wear the head wrap as a Queen might wear a crown. In this way the headwrap corresponds to African & women's manner of hair styling, whereas the hair is pulled so as to expose the forehead and is often drawn to a heightened mass on top of the head " Crown" ".
